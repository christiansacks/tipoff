DNS Servers

DNS servers used for domain checks (SPF, DMARC, DKIM, etc.). Public DNS ensures checks work correctly regardless of your local network or VPN configuration.

Default: Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) and Google (8.8.8.8). Add your internal resolver IP here if you need to check private/internal domains.

Custom VM MAC Prefixes

MAC OUI prefixes (first 3 octets) to treat as virtual machines, in addition to the built-in list. Useful for hypervisors not covered automatically, such as ESXi hosts with non-standard MACs. Format: BC:24:11 — separate multiple entries with commas.

Example Proxmox OUI: BC:24:11. Changes take effect on the next LAN scan.
